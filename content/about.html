Welcome to the Technocopia wiki!

This wiki functions as an in-depth resource for members, students and public visitors of Technocopia and its workspace.

Technocopia is a 501(c)(3) non-profit education center and maker space located in Worcester, Massachusetts. We offer unique programs in STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art and math) education for adults and youth, tool training classes, and workspaces for artisans of all kinds. Our space includes a wood shop, metal shop, classroom, lounge, multiple half-wall studio spaces, several tech-based areas, and a community workspace. Our tools range from 3D printers and soldering stations, to CNC mills and a laser cutter. Technocopia aims to be a central hub for STEAM artisans, educators, students and mentors in central Massachusetts.
